Lavendar Incense Sticks

I watch from my bedpost

at the gently lit lumpy stick that sits fixed in place

at an angled position,

hovering above the elongated wooden platform that is perched below it.

Aromas of timeless herbs

Disperse through the air

Leaving with me with nothing but

The sweetest of dreams.
