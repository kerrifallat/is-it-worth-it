The Aftermath of Rainfall



The delightful lingering aroma

Of happy tulips,

daisies

And Daffodils

Who have had a

Large Drink

permeates

through

The now

light

And fluffy textured

air.

Humidity is lifted.

The atmosphere sheds its winter coat

Bearing and embracing

Its nakedness.
