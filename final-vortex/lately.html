Lately



Lately,
I've been guzzling down three glasses of wine
and writing words that expose my soul.
Lately,
I've been finding it hard to breathe
whenever my lips attempt to eject words.
Lately,
All I've been dreaming about
is collecting rocks.
Lately,
I've been feeling contradicting emotions.
Lately,
I've been grieving over friendships
that are no longer the same.
Lately,
I've been soaking in feelings of being proud
over the work I've created.
Lately,
I've been sitting
and contemplating what is to feel lonely.
Lately,
I've been wanting to get stronger.
Lately,
I've been so tired of being tired.
Lately,
I've been okay I guess.
