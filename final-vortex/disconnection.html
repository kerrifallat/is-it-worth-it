DISCONNECTION

A swift change in perception.
A lack of a clear or
stable
direction.
Up
Down
Left and
Right,
Sideways,
Backwards,
Forwards,
Half empty
Half full
cup.

Where is the ground?
Is it down below?
Or is it burried somewhere in the sky?
I don't know.
Flowers bloom.
Flowers die.
From the ashes
when will I at long last arise?
