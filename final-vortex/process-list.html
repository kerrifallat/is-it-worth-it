A List Regarding The Process of Process

  • listening to music
  • Building visual narratives inside my mind
  • Replaying specific songs over and over again
  • Refining the storyboard in my mind with each repeat of the song
  • Going for a walk or engaging in movement while listening to these songs and visualizing the story
  • Feeling an urgency to record the thoughts in some way
  • Creating digital notes in note app on phone
  • Doodling
  • Character charts
  • Mind maps/bubble charts
  • Process visualizations
  • List making
  • Writing the first paragraph or the first string of words
  • Reading this sentence, string of words or paragraphs out loud
  • Turning to thesauruses to generate refinements specifically to the adjectives in the writing
  • Rereading the sentence several more times
  • Listening to the song again and/or referring back to recorded forms of the story (mindmaps, doodles, character charts, process visualizations, handwritten or digitally typed out lists)
  • Writing the next chunk
  • Reading this chunk of writing out loud
  • Referring once more back to the thesaurus to clean up the language
  • Reading the chunk out loud once more with refinements
  • Reading the first and freshly written chunk together
  • Referring back to the thesaurus again
  • Reading out loud both writing excerpts once more
  • Taking a break
  • Drinking some water
  • Sitting on the porch, allowing the fresh air to bring about further contemplation and inspiration
