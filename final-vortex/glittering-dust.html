GLITTERING
DUST

Octagonal shaped flakes ejected from the skies's watering mouth-

Decorating and blanketing the world simultaneously.

The once plain and bland lanscapes of the front lawn had been transfigured into a stage

where mother nature herself was displaying an artistic and poetic performance all her own.

The bare trees became fashion models

striking unique and organic poses

in their new sparkling threads.

The snow flurries also seemed to putting on a show of their own-

waltzing and dancing in good spirits with one another before

gentling trickling to the ground

where they would become merged and mashed with one another.
