GLITTERING
DUST

Octagonal shaped flakes ejected from the skies's watering mouth,

dressing the world in a fierce new fashion.

The once plain and bland landscapes of the front lawn transformed into a theatrical and grand stage

where mother nature, herself was the new, emerging star of the show.

The bare trees became iconic fashion models,

striking unique and organic poses

in their new sparkling threads.

The snow flurries became a energized groups of dancers,

waltzing and prancing, in good spirits with one another before

gentling trickling to the ground

where they would become merged and mashed with one another.
