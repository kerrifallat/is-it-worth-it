THE MATTRESS

One day a space that once embedded comfort and security
Experienced a severe malfunction.
One day a space where I once laid my head at night>br> To dream and to sleep
Twisted and transfigured into a nightmarish realm.
This realm existed between dimensions.
Outside of a linear timeframe.
It was ruled and raided by malicious monsters
And hungry demons
Who snacked and feasted upon the sweet and delicious misery
Of individuals.
With every drop of perspiration,
With every escalation in heart rates,
And swift shifts between what is considered reality and
Illusion,
Their mouths would ooze
With steady glops of
Thick drool.
