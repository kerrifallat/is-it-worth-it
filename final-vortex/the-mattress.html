THE MATTRESS

The sheets are freshly washed
But the internal stains remain.
The sheets cling to the mattress
and the mattress clings to me
like sticky
and goppy
Elmer's glue.
The kind that leaves white films on your finger tips
that some took joy in peeling off as kindergardeners.
I keep trying to flay it off
but its reminscents remain.
They linger,
The same as the toxicity that is married to this mattress.
That tightly grips this object.
