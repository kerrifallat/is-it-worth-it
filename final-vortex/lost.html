When lost one can be:

Lingering, Longing, Lonely, Lacking, Learning, Leaving, Late.


When lost one can find themselves:

Observing, Off-course, Out of place, Outside.


When lost one can feel as though they are:

Searching, seeking, slipping, sneaking, sleuthing.


When Lost one can be:

Turning, tumbling, tangled.
